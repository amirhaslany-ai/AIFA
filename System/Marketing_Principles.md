# Marketing Principles
**Document ID:** `System/Marketing_Principles.md`
**Referenced by:** `06_Marketing/README.md`

> Distinct from `06_Marketing/README.md` (the engine's process/mechanics): this file is the philosophy that process operates under — the "why this way, not that way" behind Marketing's Decision Rules, kept separate so the engine spec doesn't have to carry both process and philosophy.

## Growth vs. trust

Where a growth tactic and the honest-voice mandate (`01_Core/Vision.md`) conflict, trust wins. This isn't a balance to strike case-by-case — it's a fixed priority order. `06_Marketing/README.md`'s traceability rule (no derived asset may introduce a claim absent from the source video) is the mechanical enforcement of this principle; this file states the reasoning: a channel that trades short-term engagement for viewer trust is trading away the entire differentiation thesis this project is built on.

## Anti-patterns to avoid

- **Engagement bait** — captions/hooks that ask a question or make a claim purely to manufacture comments/shares, disconnected from the actual content.
- **Misleading thumbnails or hooks** — covered mechanically by `06_Marketing/README.md`'s traceability rule and `07_SEO/README.md`'s thumbnail honesty rule; stated here as a principle: the clip/caption/thumbnail is a preview of real content, never a promise the content doesn't keep.
- **Manufactured urgency** — false scarcity or urgency framing ("before this gets taken down," "they don't want you to know this") has no place in this channel's voice, regardless of how well it performs elsewhere.
