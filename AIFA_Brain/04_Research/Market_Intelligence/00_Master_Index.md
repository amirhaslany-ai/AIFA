## PART 4 — GLOBAL EVIDENCE REGISTER (source hierarchy)

**Tier 1 — Primary/authoritative (highest trust):** DataReportal/Kepios, World Bank, Migration Policy Institute, PAAIA/ACS (market data); company newsrooms & BusinessWire (funding); Nature Scientific Reports, arXiv (Persian benchmarks); SEC/S-1 filings; bon-bast/alanchand (FX).
**Tier 2 — Reputable secondary:** Sacra, Tracxn, TechCrunch, Reuters, Bloomberg, Similarweb, Semrush (financials/traffic — good but estimate-based).
**Tier 3 — Trade/blog/aggregator (corroborate before relying):** pricing-comparison sites, review blogs, Faradars/techtip (Iranian market color), intermediary blogs (settlement mechanics).
**Tier 4 — Self-reported/marketing (treat as claims):** platform self-claims (user counts), unverified forum posts.

**Rule:** a FACT rated High cites Tier 1–2. A claim resting only on Tier 3–4 is capped at Medium and flagged. **No conclusion in this repository rests solely on Tier 4.**
