Business decisions: pricing, business model, go-to-market, financial, legal, partnership, and risk decisions. This is where the real answers to `Platform/HANDOVER/12_OPEN_DECISIONS.md`'s open founder-level questions get formally recorded once they're actually decided (not just discussed) — e.g. the real pricing markup, the payment strategy, which AI vendor(s) to run.

Status legend: **Locked** / **Provisional** / **Superseded** (see `DECISION_LOG.md` for definitions).
