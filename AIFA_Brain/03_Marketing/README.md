## Purpose

**Company-level** marketing: positioning, brand strategy, campaign planning, channel strategy for AIFA the company/product. This is deliberately a different scope from Content Studio's own `06_Marketing/`, which is about promoting Shima's individual videos/content — not confused with each other, but genuinely related (Content Studio's output is itself a marketing channel for AIFA Platform, per Content Studio's own `06_Marketing/Platform_Derivation.md`, which documents that exact relationship from the content side).

Currently structure-only. The first real marketing documents here depend on `01_Product/` and `02_Business/Go_To_Market/` existing first — you can't position a product that hasn't been defined yet.

## Update rules

- State the target audience explicitly in every positioning document — "everyone" is not an audience.
- Distinguish brand-level marketing (this folder) from tactical content marketing (Content Studio's `06_Marketing/`) explicitly when the two interact, rather than letting the distinction blur.
- Campaign documents should link to the business goal they serve (`02_Business/Go_To_Market/`), not stand alone.
