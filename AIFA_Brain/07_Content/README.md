## Purpose

Company-level content **strategy** — what role content plays in the business overall, cross-content-type portfolio decisions, content-as-a-growth-channel planning. This is deliberately not where actual content lives or gets produced: Content Studio (`01_Core/` through `10_Orchestrator/`, `System/`) owns the entire real production pipeline, persona, and quality bar for actual scripts/videos, and this folder does not duplicate or override any of that.

Currently structure-only.

## Update rules

- This folder answers "why content, and how much, and what mix" — not "how do we write a script" (that's `04_ScriptEngine/`) or "what's Shima's voice" (that's `System/Shima_Persona.md`).
- Any strategic content decision that would actually change how Content Studio operates must be reflected back into Content Studio's own governed docs (via the Constitution's founder-approval process), not just recorded here — this folder informs strategy, it doesn't override Content Studio's governance.

## Relationships

- **Content Studio (`01_Core/`–`10_Orchestrator/`, `System/`)** — owns actual content production; this folder is upstream strategy, not a parallel production system.
- **`03_Marketing/`** — content strategy and marketing strategy overlap; cross-reference rather than duplicate.
- **`04_Research/User_Research/`** — content strategy should be grounded in who's actually watching/reading, not assumed.
