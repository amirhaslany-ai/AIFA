## Purpose

Partner relationships beyond AI vendor relationships (which live in `../../06_AI/`, since they're large and technical enough to warrant their own home) — distribution partners, integration partners, and third-party API consumers (`../../10_API/`'s eventual partners). Currently structure-only; use `TEMPLATE_Partnership_Brief.md` per partner.

## Update rules

One document per partner relationship, not one giant running list — makes it easy to archive a specific relationship (to `../../16_Archive/`) without disturbing the others. Keep status current: an inactive or ended partnership should be marked and archived promptly, not left looking live.
